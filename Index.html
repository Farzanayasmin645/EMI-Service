Background of the EMI Project.

Health is the most important asset that one has, and it deserves the utmost protection and care. Medical-related emergencies can occur at any time, and the last thing you want to worry about is how to pay for your care. That's where medical loans can be useful. Medical loans are unsecured personal loans that you can avail to cover sudden and unforeseen medical expenses. These expenses can include hospital bills, prescription bills, surgery costs, chemotherapy costs, etc. Moreover, a medical emergency loan can ensure that you receive the best treatment possible and clear out all bills in case of a cash crunch.(Kotak Mahindra Bank, 2022).
