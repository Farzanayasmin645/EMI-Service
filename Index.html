Background of the EMI Project

Health is the most important asset that one has, and it deserves the utmost protection and care. Medical-related emergencies can occur at any time, and the last thing you want to worry about is how to pay for your care. That's where medical loans can be useful. Medical loans are unsecured personal loans that you can avail to cover sudden and unforeseen medical expenses. These expenses can include hospital bills, prescription bills, surgery costs, chemotherapy costs, etc. Moreover, a medical emergency loan can ensure that you receive the best treatment possible and clear out all bills in case of a cash crunch.(Kotak Mahindra Bank, 2022). For immediate cases patients' family members will get some other benefit for the EMI system. Such as, a medical emergency loan does not require you to provide collateral to approve your loan. Also, process without hassles or stress.

In Addition, you can choose an Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) that is affordable for you because a medical loan has a flexible repayment period. Simply use an EMI calculator for a personal loan to determine how much you can afford to pay each month. Based on the necessary loan amount, this tool will assist you in calculating your monthly payment. For example, EMI from a hospital is offered on condition to their client, you have to repay that money with a very small amount of internet. Suppose, if you take 1 lakh money from the hospital you have to repay within how many months you can decide by yourself and pay it back by every month. Including this money you also have to give very little percentage interest to the hospital. (Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) Definition, 2021).

In our Bangladesh if we see then we will find lots of poor people dying because they don't have money for their treatment. Therefore, EMI systems help people significantly if there is an emergency.
